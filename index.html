What we do

Innovate Highfliers Consulting Ltd, established in 2019, empowers individuals and organizations through comprehensive services, including top-notch training, specialized capacity building, and strategic consultancy in areas such as entrepreneurship, human trafficking solutions, and gender-based violence awareness

  • Capacity building

    Training and Development: Designing and delivering training programs to improve the skills and knowledge of individuals and teams within an organization.
    Leadership Development: Offering programs to cultivate leadership skills among executives and managers.
    Organizational Development: Assisting in the development and implementation of strategies to improve overall organizational effectiveness.

  • Management consulting

    Strategic Planning: Assisting organizations in developing long-term strategies to achieve their goals.
    Operational Improvement: Identifying and implementing process improvements to enhance efficiency and effectiveness.
    Change Management: Helping organizations navigate and manage change initiatives within their structures.
    Financial Management: Providing advice on financial strategies, budgeting, and financial analysis.
    Human Resources Consulting: Assisting with HR-related issues, including talent management, employee engagement, and workforce planning.

  • Advisory Services

    Policy Advisory: Offering guidance on policy formulation and implementation.
    Technology Advisory: Providing advice on the adoption and integration of new technologies.
    Risk Management: Assessing and managing risks associated with various aspects of business operations.

  • Capacity Assessment

    Organizational Assessments: Evaluating an organization's current capabilities, strengths, and weaknesses.
    Training Needs Assessment: Identifying skill gaps and developing plans to address them through training initiatives.

  • Project Management

    Project Planning: Assisting in the development of project plans and timelines.
    Project Evaluation: Conducting evaluations to assess the success and impact of projects.
    Networking and Collaboration: Facilitating Partnerships: Helping organizations connect with potential collaborators, partners, and stakeholders.
    Networking Events: Hosting events to facilitate networking opportunities among professionals and organizations.
    Documentation and Reporting: Assisting organizations in documenting and reporting their activities and outcomes.
    Knowledge Sharing: Facilitating the exchange of knowledge and best practices within and between organizations.
    Market Research: Conducting studies to analyse market trends, customer preferences, and competitor activities.
