What we do

Innovate Highfliers Consulting Ltd, established in 2019, empowers individuals and organizations through comprehensive services, including top-notch training, specialized capacity building, and strategic consultancy in areas such as entrepreneurship, human trafficking solutions, and gender-based violence awareness

  • Training Excellence

    Providing top-notch training in entrepreneurship, skills acquisition, workplace ethics, and management practices.

  • Human Trafficking Solutions

    Specialized capacity building aligned with the National Action Plan on Human Trafficking in Nigeria

  • GBV Awareness & Research

    Initiating awareness campaigns and research programs addressing Gender-Based Violence (GBV) and supporting victims.

  • Strategic Business Consultancy

    Expert consultancy in business development, feasibility studies, analysis, evaluation, and comprehensive planning.

  • Corporate Mobilization

    Mobilizing consulting service providers, coordinating, empowering, and ensuring high-quality services for businesses and organizations.

  • Property Intermediary Services

    Facilitating property transactions, serving as trusted intermediaries in the sale, purchase, and legal transfer of property ownership.
